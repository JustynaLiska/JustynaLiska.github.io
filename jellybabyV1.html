    Create a Title Sequence for NFS student
    2017

    The short film production ‘Jelly Baby’ made by Naomi Fagan - a graduate of National Film Shcool in IADT, tells a story about young girl who gave birth at the age of seventeen. The plot focuses on mother and daughter relationship, emphasising strong connection and their everyday struggle. The arrogant nature, naive and immature approach to their lives are the primary characteristic used by Naomi.

    Careless, naivety and clumsy - these are features
    I wanted to acquaint to the audience. Think about
    it as a story of a child who decided to have her five minutes of spark. Dressing up in her mom's clothes, putting colourful makeup on and dancing in front
    of the camera like there is no tomorrow. PURE FUN!

    Production:

    Black background, fluorescent light + paints, 60 fps camera and stable tripod. It is challenging to work with dark areas, especially in college studio. That is why post-production plays a significant role. After hundred hours of reshooting there is a fun part of cutting, reversing, repeating and mixing. Then, you only have to pray your computer will not explode while rendering the video :)

    Poster:

    That is where it all has started. I had many different ideas - very pink and soft ideas... And then BOOM!
    A little bit of ink and there we go! A perfectly vibrant colours on pitch black background! In case you wonder - no I wasn't drawing on black paper, again, post-production can do magic!

    Packaging:

    After the production, packaging it is only a formality. Selecting materials, cutting, pasting, prototyping...
    It may be a nice little break from the computer.
