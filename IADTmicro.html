IADT Microsite

IADT Portfolio Guidance

UI & UX Design, HTML + CSS

  • Client:

  • Aim:

  • Year:

    • IADT
    Design and code a microsite
    2016

    The main aim of this project was to create a responsive microsite to help incoming students understand the portfolio requirements for IADT and inspire them in future portfolio creations. I came up with a solution of three steps system keeping everything clear, simple and understandable.

    Modern and contrasting colours of the site with the usage of simple filters help incoming students not only with gaining the right informaton but also in choosing the adequate for them course.

    Research:

    Before I have started the design part, I had to understand what is the aim of the website. Who is the audience? What are the most critical features students should know when submitting and collecting portfolio? I had applied to the IADT three years ago, which means I had to refresh my memory and gather some information.


    Process:

    First I had spoken with the lectures from different courses, to find out what are the exact requirements for the portfolio. After that, I spoke with younger students to find out what were the biggest issues for them when collecting their work.


    Solution:

    The research was done, all the content created, time to design! Whith piles of paper content in front of me I came up one rule - it has to be simple as a piece of cake! Afterall, you don't have to be second Michelangelo to get the place, so here you go - 3 easy steps to become an IADT student!
