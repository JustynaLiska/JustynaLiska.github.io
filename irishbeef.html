    Identification and Exhibition Space
    2016

    The objective of this project was to create an identity design and exhibition space for Irish Beef - Bord Bia based in Dublin Ireland. The client requirement was to promote Irish Beef not only to local buyers and customers but also to Asia countries.

    As a product by itself, Irish Beed is well known for its excellent quality, due to the climate, landscapes and protectiveness of Irishâ€™s farmers. Our client wanted to promote the product to other countries by memorable identify and exhibition.

    Logo:

    The outcome is a mix of traditional and modern brand identification kept in light-green colour, which represents fresh Irish pastures. Moreover, hand-made elements - such as stamped logo, stand for great quality and stability of the product.

    Materials:

    The attention to details is significant that is why I have selected to use high-quality materials. For booklet, I have used thick, rugged paper which reflects the sustainability of the product. In addition to the booklet, I have also managed to create an actual grocery bag with a usage of screen - printing technique.

    Exhibition:

    Besides the identification, we were asked to design an exhibition space. For that, it was necessary to visit the place, take a lot of pictured and get few building plans. To better understand my idea I had created a 3d model out of foam core and paper.

    Again, the use of ecological materials is the majority. Tha is why I have decided to use plywood for the floor. Also for an optical illusion, I have decided to paint the common green shape at few spots. Figures of cows in size of a human play an educational role and attract people from the outside. Everything is kept in bright and friendly for eyes colours.
